About Tail Wags

Dog breeders and trainers are some of the most painstaking, conscientious and generous people I came across. Regrettably, most are burned out and break down because their passion, and especially raising healthy furbabies is completely separate from the actual business side of running a daycare. To add insult to injury, there is no leading resource to help the industry solve their business problems. Until now…

We believe they are incredibly altruistic to their community. Indeed, people from anywhere and everywhere come to breeders and trainers to find and adopt their companion of a lifetime. Breeders/trainers truly represent love and care but this time it is our turn to help them because they deserve it so much.

Our mission is to empower you as a strong leader of your own community. We’ll bring to light efficient online marketing strategies, offline community organizing tips, smart branding moves, and more that will make your business reputable and worth talking about. I want to help breeding business owners and carers like you build a thriving and profitable business that brings you boundless satisfaction and supports your ideal lifestyle.
